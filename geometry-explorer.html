What is Geometry?

The study of shapes, sizes, and the spaces around us.

🌍 What is Geometry?

Geometry comes from Greek words meaning "Earth measurement." It's the study of points, lines, shapes, angles, and spaces. Everything around you has geometry!

📐 Why Does It Matter?

Architects use geometry to design buildings. Engineers use it to build bridges. Artists use it to create beautiful compositions. Even nature follows geometric patterns!

✨ Where We See It

Hexagons in honeycomb, spirals in galaxies, circles in wheels, triangles in trusses, rectangles everywhere! Geometry is the hidden language of the universe.

Angle Types Reference

Here's how we classify angles by their size:

Acute: 0° – 90°

Sharp, pointy angles. Any angle smaller than a right angle. Think of an arrow tip or a slice of pizza.

Right: 90°

A perfect corner, like the corners of a square or a door. We mark it with a small square symbol.

Obtuse: 90° – 180°

Wider, blunter angles. More than a right angle but not yet a straight line. Like an open book.

Straight: 180°

A perfectly flat line with no bend. Like opening a book all the way flat. This is half a full rotation.

Reflex: 180° – 360°

The "outside" angle. More than straight but less than a full circle. It's the angle you measure the long way around.

Full: 360°

A complete rotation, like spinning around in a full circle and facing the same direction again.

12 Amazing Geometry Facts

Discover the cool side of shapes and patterns!

🎯
Circles are Special
A circle has infinite symmetry lines! No matter where you draw a line through the center, both sides are mirror images.
🏗️
Triangles are Strongest
Triangles are the strongest shapes. That's why bridges, towers, and roofs use triangular bracing to stay up!
🐝
Hexagon Power
Bees build honeycomb with hexagons because they're the most efficient shape for storing the most honey using the least wax.
🌊
The Golden Ratio
Nature loves the number 1.618 (phi). It appears in spirals, flowers, sea shells, and even your own body! It's the most beautiful proportion.
⭐
5-Point Stars
The 5-pointed star appears everywhere in nature — from starfish to flowers. It has perfect rotational symmetry!
🔷
Angles in Triangles
No matter what triangle you draw, the three angles always add up to exactly 180 degrees. Always! It's a guaranteed rule.
🌍
Earth is Roughly Spherical
A sphere is the most efficient shape for distributing weight evenly. That's why planets and stars are spheres!
📐
Pythagorean Theorem
In any right triangle, a² + b² = c². This simple formula is used in architecture, engineering, and computer graphics!
🎨
Art Loves Geometry
Famous artists like Picasso and Da Vinci used geometric shapes as the foundation for their most famous works.
🕐
Clock Geometry
A clock face is divided into 12 equal sections. Each hour is 30 degrees (360° ÷ 12 = 30°).
🎮
Video Games Use Geometry
Every 3D video game is made from millions of triangles. The computer calculates light, shadows, and movement using pure geometry!
✨
Snowflakes are Geometric
Every snowflake has 6-fold rotational symmetry. No two are identical, but they all follow the same geometric rules!
